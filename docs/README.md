# Scribe Documentation

Scribe lives in your menu bar and turns speech into text, entirely on your Mac.
Press a keyboard shortcut, talk, press it again — the transcript is saved and,
in Dictation mode, pasted right where your cursor is. No cloud, no accounts, no audio ever leaves your
machine.
